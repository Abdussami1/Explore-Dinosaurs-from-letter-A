Aardonyx

Aardonyx was only describe for the first time in 2009.
No image of how it would have looked are available yet.
Type of Dinosaur: Prosauropod
Length: 8.0m
Diet: Herbivorous
When it lived: Early Jurassic, 199-189 million years ago
Found in: South Africa
Aardonyx is known from 2 immature individuals. Adults would have been much larger, probably over 10m.
Aardonyx would have been largely bipedal (walking on 2 legs) but also capable of walking on all 4 legs.
This and its way of feeding are transitional features between those of basal sauropodomorphs and the more derived sauropods (large dinosaurs that walked on all fours) that came later.
